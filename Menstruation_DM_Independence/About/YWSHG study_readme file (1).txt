Description of methods used for collection/generation of data: <Include links or references to publications or other documentation containing experimental design or protocols used in data collection>The study followed a onetime cross-sectional survey design. Semi-structured bilingual (Hindi and English) questionnaire was used to interview unmarried adolescent girls and young women in two districts of Uttar Pradesh. Data was collected by trained female research assistants using computer assisted personal interviewing (CAPI) techniques. 2. Methods for processing the data: <describe how the submitted data were generated from the raw or collected data> The data entry package was developed in CSPro 6.3 with inbuilt logics and checks to minimize errors. The collected was converted into SPSS version 16.0. All open-ended responses were scrutinized as part of data preparation.  3. Describe any quality-assurance procedures performed on the data: The data entry package used in the CAPI had inbuilt logics and checks to minimize errors. The field supervisors sent data daily to study coordinator who analyzed data on an ongoing basis and discussed any data related queries with the field teams. 7.
